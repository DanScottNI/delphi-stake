Stake ~ Introduction

Stake is a Windows-based level editor for the NES ROM, Castlevania. It was developed using Delphi 6, on the Windows XP operating system. It supports the many versions of Castlevania, such as the european ROM, the american ROM, and even Akumajou Dracula - the special rerelease of the Japanese CV. It doesn't however, support the Famicom Disk System original of CV. (Yet :)) Here's what features Stake has:

- Graphics are read from the ROM.
- Full level editing of all levels in the ROM.
- TSA editing
- Level palette editing
- Stairs editing
- Doors editing
- Entrances editing
- Basic enemy editing
- Basic 8x8 Tile Editor.

It doesn't support the following important things yet:

- Hidden items editing
- Floating platform editing
